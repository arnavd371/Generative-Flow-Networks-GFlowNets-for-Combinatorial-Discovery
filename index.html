GFlowNets for Combinatorial Discovery

Minimal research-style demo: generate diverse high-reward discrete objects (graphs and sorting networks).

Paradigm Shift

Standard RL seeks one best trajectory; GFlowNets sample many trajectories proportional to terminal reward.

This supports exploration across multiple modes instead of collapse to one local optimum.

Math Core

Flow conservation on a state DAG:

P_F(s → s′) F(s) = P_B(s′ → s) F(s′)

Enforced via detailed balance or trajectory balance objectives.

Research Angle

Search for graph-theory counterexamples and non-standard optimized sorting networks with diverse candidate generation.
